## 💡 Senior-Level Decisions

### **Why Team Portfolio Focus?**

1. **Clear Identity:**
   - Immediately shows it's a TEAM
   - Not a personal portfolio
   - Professional branding

2. **Better Positioning:**
   - "DevDuo" = memorable brand
   - "Full-Stack Team" = clear value proposition
   - Team capabilities highlighted

3. **User Expectations:**
   - Users know they're hiring a TEAM
   - Not just one person
   - Full-stack capabilities

4. **SEO Benefits:**
   - "Full-Stack Development Team"
   - "DevDuo Team"
   - Better search visibility

5. **Scalability:**
   - Easy to add more team members
   - Team brand grows
   - Not tied to one person

---

## 🎯 Key Messages

### **What Users See:**

1. **Brand:** DevDuo (professional team name)
